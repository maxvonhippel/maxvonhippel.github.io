Hi! My name is Max von Hippel

... and I am a PhD student at Northeastern University Khoury College of Computer Science, advised by Dr. Cristina Nita-Rotaru in the NDS2 group. I have a BS in pure mathematics from the University of Arizona, and an International IB diploma from UWCCR. My research focuses on the automatic discovery of attacks against protocols, using formal methods. In my free-time, I help run the Boston Computation Club. You can also find me on Twitter or Mastodon, or email me at maxvh-at-hey-dot-com. My old website is archived here.
